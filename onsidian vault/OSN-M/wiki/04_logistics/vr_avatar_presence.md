# VR Avatar Presence

VR Avatar Presence is the "Sense of Togetherness" elicited by 3D representations of performers in a shared virtual space.

## Components
- **Spatial Audio:** Hearing a singer "to the left" or "behind" enhances the choral blend perception.
- **IK Tracking:** Inverse Kinematics (IK) makes body language feel more natural than static 2D icons.
- **Attentional Focus:** The ability to physically turn one's head to look at a soloist or the conductor.

## Relation to E(t)
Presence is the **Subjective Albedo** stage of Entanglement. High presence scores in user surveys should correlate with high Phase-Locking Values (PLV) in our head-tracking data. If presence is high but synchronization is low, the system is in a state of **Illusory Entanglement**.

# Prof. Peter Gloor

**Role**: Second Grader / Methodological Framework Architect (Uni Köln / ex-MIT)

## Relevance to Project
Prof. Gloor is the creator of the Collaborative Innovation Networks (COINs) framework and the concept of Cybernetic Alchemy. He expects our project to view online choirs through the lens of COINs, treating the choir as a network expressing itself through voice.

## Key Theoretical Contributions
- **Social Entanglement**: Adapted the concept of quantum entanglement to human communication, demonstrating that individuals who share synchronized rhythms (measured via dynamic social network analysis) experience higher team flow and performance (see [[s_02_entanglement]]).
- **Cybernetic Alchemy**: A four-stage framework (Nigredo, Albedo, Citrinitas, Rubedo) for transforming raw interaction data into synthesized wisdom. Our vault strictly follows this structure.
- **Honest Signals**: Building on Pentland's work (MIT 2008), measuring unconscious biological and behavioral signals.

## Expected Deliverables
- An **alchemical-stage Honest-Signals diagram** showing how raw NMP data transforms into meaning.
- Application of the COINs methodology to our own team via a **Virtual Mirror** (WhatsApp analysis using SocialCompass).

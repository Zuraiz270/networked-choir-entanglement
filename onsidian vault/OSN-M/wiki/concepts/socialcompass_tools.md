# SocialCompass Tools

The **SocialCompass** suite is a set of tools developed by **[[Peter_Gloor]]** and his team to measure and visualize communication patterns and behavioral "honest signals" within Collaborative Innovation Networks (COINs).

## The Four Tools
1. **Symbiont**: Analyzes group communication and information flow (e.g., email or chat archives).
2. **SC Chat**: Real-time analysis of chat interactions to detect emotional state and responsiveness.
3. **Perceptiface**: Uses computer vision to analyze facial expressions and non-verbal cues during video meetings.
4. **Beecome**: A platform for visualizing group evolution and individual roles (Bees, Ants, Butterflies, etc.).

## Application in SNA-OSN-M
We use these tools to perform the **Virtual Mirror** iteration (scheduled for May 21), exporting our team WhatsApp logs into Symbiont to measure our own internal synchronization and leadership dynamics.

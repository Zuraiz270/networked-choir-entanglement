# Lawful Access Prerequisite

"Lawful Access" is a mandatory condition in most international TDM copyright exceptions (notably EU Articles 3 & 4). It means the researcher must have a legal right to view or access the data before mining it.

## Scope
- **Open Access:** Works under Creative Commons or Public Domain.
- **Subscriptions:** Institutional access to academic databases (IEEE Xplore, ACM).
- **Public Platforms:** Publicly available content on platforms like YouTube (subject to Terms of Service).

## In SNA-OSN-M
To ensure compliance, our data mining pipeline only scrapes **Publicly Available** videos or datasets where we have institutional credentials. We strictly avoid "circumventing technical protection measures" (e.g., hacking paywalls or bypassing DRM), as this would invalidate our TDM research exception.

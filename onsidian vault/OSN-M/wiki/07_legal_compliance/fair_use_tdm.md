# Fair Use in TDM

Fair Use is a legal doctrine that promotes freedom of expression by permitting the unlicensed use of copyright-protected works under certain circumstances.

## The 4-Step Test (US Model)
1. **Purpose and Character:** Is the use transformative? (e.g., turning a video into a mathematical index $E(t)$).
2. **Nature of Work:** Is the source data factual or highly creative?
3. **Amount/Substantiality:** How much of the original work is used?
4. **Market Effect:** Does the use replace the original in the market?

## Application to SNA-OSN-M
Our project is highly transformative. We do not redistribute the original musical works; we extract "metadata" (latency, synchronization indices) which does not compete with the original performance's market value. This aligns with the "Transformative Use" precedent established in *Authors Guild v. Google, Inc.* (Google Books).

# Perceived Coherence Index

The Perceived Coherence Index (PCI) measures how "unified" a remote musical environment feels to the performer.

## Sub-Metrics (P-23)
- **Consistency:** Similarity of the virtual space to a real-world rehearsal room.
- **Social Presence:** The "Same Room" feeling.
- **Immersion:** The degree of involvement in the virtual performance space.
- **Naturalness:** The ease of non-verbal communication.

## Implementation in SNA-OSN-M
PCI serves as the **Subjective Ground Truth** for our $E(t)$ formula. We use it to calibrate the weights of our technical metrics. If $E(t)$ is high but PCI is low, it suggests the system is technically synchronized but "Aesthetically Uncanny."

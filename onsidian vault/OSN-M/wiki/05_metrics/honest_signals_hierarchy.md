# Honest Signals Hierarchy

The Honest Signals Hierarchy (S-01) categorizes the different types of non-verbal data we extract to calculate the **Entanglement Index $E(t)$**.

## Levels of the Hierarchy
1. **Biological Layer:** Respiration rate (P-16), heart rate variability, bioelectrics. (The "Ground Truth" of life).
2. **Kinetic Layer:** Body pose (P-03), gesture synchrony (P-08), hierarchical motion (P-19). (The "Visual Signal").
3. **Acoustic Layer:** Vocal pitch salience (P-02), prosody, latency/response timing (P-15). (The "Auditory Signal").
4. **Network Layer:** Influence mapping (P-21), causal graphs (P-22), social presence (P-23). (The "Emergent Structure").

## Implementation
Our platform calculates a weighted average across these levels. A high $E(t)$ requires **Cross-Layer Coherence**—e.g., the breathing rhythm must predict the visual attack, which must align with the auditory onset.
